// how it works

From idea to live site.

Five steps. No surprises. Your site, done right.

01 /

Discovery

We kick off with a call to understand your goals, audience, and vision. No templates, no assumptions — just a real conversation about what you need and what will make your site convert.

02 /

Mockup

Before a single line of code, you get a visual preview of the site. We nail the look, feel, and layout until it's exactly right — changes here are free, changes later aren't.

03 /

Build

With the design locked in, I get to work. Clean code, fast load times, mobile-first from day one. Built to perform, not just to look good in a screenshot.

04 /

Revisions

You get a full round of revisions to fine-tune anything that isn't exactly right. This is your site — it should feel exactly like you envisioned it.

05 /

Launch

We go live together. You get the files, the credentials, and everything you need to own your site outright. No lock-in, no ongoing fees — just a site that works.
